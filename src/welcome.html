🛡️ Welcome to AegisGate Lens

The privacy-first browser extension that protects your prompts from leaking sensitive data to AI providers.

The 5 promises

1. No prompt content ever leaves your browser. The Lens's privacy boundary is structural: the content script is the only module that sees your prompt text, and it never sends it anywhere.
2. Telemetry is opt-in. Default is OFF. By default, the Lens detects locally only and shows you a warning. It sends nothing to any server. To contribute anonymized detection metadata (which improves detection for everyone), toggle the opt-in.
3. The 9 fields, and only the 9 fields. When you opt in, the only data sent is: domain_hash, category, severity, user_action, timestamp, model_version, lens_version, confidence, and an optional client-side ID for dedup. No URL, no prompt, no page content, no user ID.
4. Open source. Audited. Reproducible. The Lens is Apache 2.0. The build tool, the backend, and every dep are open and reproducible. Zero third-party JavaScript dependencies.
5. You can delete everything, anytime. The local audit log is in your browser. The backend purges raw events after 90 days, and "send anyway" events after 24 hours. Email privacy@aegisgatesecurity.io to request deletion.

Get started

The Lens is now active on supported AI providers (chat.openai.com, chatgpt.com, claude.ai, gemini.google.com, copilot.microsoft.com). Try typing an email address into a prompt and see the warning.

Read the full privacy policy before opting in. You can change your mind at any time from the popup.
